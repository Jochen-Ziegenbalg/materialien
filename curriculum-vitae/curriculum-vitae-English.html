Jochen Ziegenbalg
curriculum vitae

  • Year of Birth: 1944 / Place: Dresden (Germany)
  • Academic life
    • German Abitur:  Leibniz Gymnasium, Stuttgart-Feuerbach, April 1964
    • 1965-1972 Studies of Mathematik, at the Universities Stuttgart und Tübingen.
    • 1972 Academic degree "Dr.rer.nat." (Universität Tübingen)
      Doctoral thesis: Periodische Normalteiler von Gruppenbasen isomorpher Gruppenringe (Periodic normal subgroups of group bases of isomorphic group rings)
    • 1975-1987 Professor for Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Education, Reutlingen
    • From 1987-2009 (retirement): Professor for Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Education, Karlsruhe

      • Positions held at the University of Education, Karlsruhe
      • Head of the Section Informatik (Computer Science) in the Department of Mathematics
      • Head of the University Budget Commission
      • Prodekan (Vice Dean) of Faculty III (Natural Sciences and Mathematics)
      • Prorektor (U.S.: Vice President / AUS: Deputy Vice Chancellor) for Research and Innovation
